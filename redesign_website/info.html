COVID-19 UPDATES


We're commited to keeping our customers and staff safe. Check here for the latest updates due to COVID-19, including the TTC's mandatory vaccination policy. Getting vaccinated remains one of the most important ways we can protect each other as well as our community. There will be Vaccination Clinics in Subway Stations, see Vaccination Clinics for further details. That is why we have made COVID-19 vaccines mandatory for all TTC employees, contractors and students.

COPPER TESTING


The TTC is participating in a joint study to test whether copper kills bacteria on high-touch surfaces. The project is the result of a pertnership between the following organizations: Teck Resources Limited, Toronto Transit Commission, TransLink, Vancouver Coastal Health (VCH), Mount Sinai Hospital, Coalition for Healthcare Acquired Infection Reduction (CHAIR), UBC Department for Materials Engineering, VGH and UBC Hospital Foundation and Westech Cleaning Audit Systems.

VACCINATION CLINICS


The TTC has partnered with East Toronto Health Partners (ETHP), Toronto Public Health (TPH) and Humber River Hospital (HRH) to provide pop-up vaccination clinics conveniently located for our customers in subway stations. The clinics are open to anyone born in 2009 or earlier for both first and second doses. No appointment is required. Moderna and Pfizer (first and second doses) will be available while supplies last each day. Future vaccination clinics are being planned.
