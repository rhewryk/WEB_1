Art Show—Featuring the works of Georgia O'Keefe, Henri Matisse. Andy Warhol and Pablo Picaso. This collection of work depicts the contrasting themes and styles of art with each other while maintaining a unified concept. Variou art movements are referenced in the work from Modernism and Fauvism to Cubism and Pop Art. A main theme of colour is greatly referenced in all of the collections of work being shown in addition to their various approaches, methods and processes of creating.

Of Modern—This unique collection of artwork will only be shown together three times. Starting in Spain at the Guggenheim Museum Bilbao in Bilbao, Spain the collection moves across Europe to France to the Musée d'art Moderne André Malraux in Le Harve, France. Finally, the work is moved to America where it is shown at the Museum Of Modern Art (MOMA) in New York City. Altogether, this collection of work is shown in three different museums for two weeks in each location. The museums in which this artwork will be shown will all differ in their curation. This is purposefully done so that each experience with Georgia O'Keeffe's, Henri Matisse's, Andy Warhol's and Pablo Picasso's work can have a stronger connection in the museum in which it is being shown.

Art—The dates of when this collection of work will be shown to the public at its three locations are as follows: Guggenheim Museum Bilbao Spain: 9-22nd of May, 2022; Musée d'art Moderne André Malraux Le Harve France: 6-19th of June, 2022; Museum Of Modern Art New York City, USA: 11-17th of July, 2022. As these dates approach, more information will be released. Until then, each individual artist's work can be viewed in the museums that have them as part of their permanent collection.
